|||
| :--------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

| **Ranged** | [Crossbow](/item/light-crossbow), [Net](/item/net), [Spear](/item/spear), [Trident](/item/trident), [Dart](/item/dart) - Attacks beyond normal range automatically miss.|
