### Stunned
 [Home](dm-operations-center) > [Conditions](conditions) > Stunned {.small-text}

- Creature is incapacitated.
- Cannot move, but can speak falteringly.
- Fail all STR & DEX saving throws.
- Attacks against have advantage.
{.square}
