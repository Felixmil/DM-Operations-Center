### Combining Effects
[Home](dm-operations-center) > [Spellcasting](spellcasting-menu) > Combining Effects {.small-text}

**Durations Overlap**. The durations of two spells with the same effect run concurrently.

**Effects Do Not Stack**. A creature cannot be double blessed or add bonuses together.

**Highest Effect Has Precedence**. The effect with the higher bonus or better benefit always has precedence over a lesser benefit.
